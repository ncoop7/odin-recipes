Pogos

The Ultimate Comfort Food

Description

Indulge in the carnival spirit with our mouthwatering Pogos! Picture this: savory sausages hugged by a crispy, golden batter. One bite brings back memories of fun fairs and outdoor festivities. Whether reliving those moments or creating new ones at home, Pogos are the perfect blend of convenience and flavor. Join me on a culinary journey that's both nostalgic and irresistibly delicious.

Ingredients

  • Frozen Pogos
  • Ketchup

Steps

  1. Remove Pogos from package in freezer
  2. Place paper towel in microwave
  3. Place Pogos on the paper towel that is now in the microwave
  4. Cook Pogos for 1 minute 5 seconds
  5. Flip Pogos 180 degrees
  6. Cook for 1 minute
  7. Remove cooked pogos, serve with ketchup and enjoy!
