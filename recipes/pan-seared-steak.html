Ingredients

  • Steak - cut of your choosing
  • Butter
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • 3 Garlic cloves
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. After letting steak sit at room temp for 15 to 20 minutes baste with olive oil
  2. Apply a liberal amount of salt to each stide of the steak
  3. Repeat with pepper
  4. Preheat pan
  5. Pour about 1 tsp of olive oil on the pan and disperse evenly
  6. Place steak in pan and sear for 1 minute per side - this includes the edges of your steak
  7. Once all sides are seared reduce heat and add your aromatics (butter and garlic)
  8. Cook until desired temperature while basting the meleted butter over the steak
