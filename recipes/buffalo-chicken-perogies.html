Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Franks Red Hot Buffalo Sauce
  • Shredded cheddar cheese
  • Butter
  • Perogies
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Gather ingredients
  2. Preheat oven to 425
  3. Cook perogies on pan with olive oil or butter
  4. Melt butter in a bowl and mix with Franks until consistency is even
  5. Shred chicken and mix with the Franks/butter combination
  6. Spread sauced up chicken on top of cooked perogies on a baking sheet
  7. Layer with cheese
  8. Place in oven and cook until desired level of melted cheese is reached
